About CanvasEdit

Powerful online image editor and collage builder written in HTML5. Here are the highlights:

  • Supports multiple images, webcam photos, text, shapes, and custom svg
  • Includes many image effects like greyscale, opacity, brightness, etc
  • Preset Instagram filters (1977, Brannan, Gotham, Hefe, etc)
  • Mousewheel zoom, custom zoom, custom scaling, rotation/angle control and object cropping, clipping, and cloning
  • History tracking with undo and redo functionality
  • Save to PNG or JPEG

To learn more, check out the article.
